Hero Section Flash Fix Test

Fix Status: Successfully implemented changes to prevent hero section flashing on page load.

Changes Made

The following modifications were implemented to fix the hero section flashing issue:

1. LandingPage Component

  • Added isInitialLoad state to prevent premature hero rendering
  • Modified hero rendering to only occur after initial load completes
  • Reduced transition pause from 500ms to 200ms for smoother transitions
  • Improved error handling to skip dissolve sequence on video errors

2. LoadingOverlay Component

  • Removed unnecessary 1.5 second minimum display time buffer
  • Simplified loading indicator visibility conditions
  • Eliminated delays that were causing the flash effect

3. LandingHero Component

  • Improved transition timing with explicit easing
  • Maintained initial hidden state to prevent flash

How to Test

  1. Open the application at http://localhost:5173
  2. Observe the page load sequence
  3. Verify that the hero section does NOT flash briefly before the video intro
  4. Confirm smooth transition from video to hero after the intro completes
  5. Test with browser refresh (Ctrl+F5) to check caching behavior

Expected Behavior

  • No brief flash of hero content on initial page load
  • Smooth video intro plays without interruption
  • Hero section fades in gracefully after video completes
  • No white screen or blank gaps during transitions
  • Hero remains visible after video intro completes
Note: The fix prevents the hero section from rendering during the initial load phase, eliminating the flash that was occurring when the component would briefly appear before being hidden by the video overlay.
